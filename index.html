Biography

I am a research associate professor in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University. I received my Ph.D. degree from Wuhan University in 2017. Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees both from Wuhan University. From 2015 to 2016, I twice worked as a research assistant in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). My research interests are in computational photography, computer graphics, and computer vision.
